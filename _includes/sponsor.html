Marchup Sponsorship Program

Supporting student events and initiatives that align with our mission.

Welcome to Marchup's sponsorship program, where we support events and initiatives that align with our mission to empower students and foster professional development. Below, you'll find our sponsorship tiers, as we provide financial assistance based on the size and scope of your event.

Size of Event/Club/Initiative Sponsorship Level Notes
Less than 50 participants $200 Upfront Payment
Between 50 and 100 participants Additional $300 Additional payment based on actual registration count on Marchup platform
For every 100 additional participants above 100 Additional $100 Additional payment based on actual registration count on Marchup platform

Maximum sponsorship of $1000.

Scenario 1: If you have 125 participants, you will get $200 upfront and an additional $300 after all participants have registered on Marchup.

Scenario 2: If you have 350 participants, you will get $200 upfront and an additional $500 after all participants have registered on Marchup.

As part of our sponsorship terms, we require that sponsored events be hosted on the Marchup platform. Organizers will create a dedicated space for the event, allowing participants to set up child spaces for their projects. This approach increases engagement on our platform and streamlines organization and consistency for event organizers. We will also help with marketing by promoting your event through our LinkedIn, Instagram, and Marchup communities, to maximize visibility and participation.
